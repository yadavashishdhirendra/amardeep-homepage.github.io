Carbon Steel Fasteners

ASTM / ASME A/SA 193 / 194 Carbon Steel Fasteners Manufacturer, Exporter & Suppliers.

Carbon Steel Bolts, Carbon Steel Anchor Fasteners, Carbon Steel Washers, CS Socket Screw Suppliers.

We are one of the leading Manufacturer, Exporter and Supplier of High quality Carbon Steel Fasteners in mumbai, India. We also offer a complete range of Carbon Steel Fasteners such as Bolts, Nuts, Washer, Screw, threaded rod and stainless screws etc. These Carbon Steel Fasteners are widely used in various application industry. These Carbon Steel Fasteners are available in different size, shapes, grades, specification & thickness as per the clients requirments. We provide products as per DIN, ANSI, JIS standards.
